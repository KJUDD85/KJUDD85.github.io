Hobby Farm

What is a hobby farm

A hobby farm is a smallholding or small farm that is maintained without expectation of being a primary source of income.

A hobby farm is usually 10 acres or less.


How to start a Hobby Farm

  1. Reseach
  2. Plant a Garden
  3. Invest in Livestock
  4. Find ways to save Money

Tips For Getting Started With Hobby Farming

  • Set a budget
  • Do your Research
  • Be patient
  • Stick with one thing at a time
  • Keep a journal
